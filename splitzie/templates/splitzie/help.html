Usage tips

Custom repayment

The repayment form lets you enter arbitrary repayments. Say you owe 2 euros to Bob and 3 euros to Alice. Instead of repaying both of them, you could also give 5 euros to Bob and ask him to pay the remainder forward. On this website you can enter this as a repayment of 5 euros to Bob. The balances will then change to reflect that Bob now owes 3 euros to Alice.

Security

Anyone who has the link of a group, can edit it. So, if a link is compromised, a malicious user could enter new payments. However, it is not possible to delete entered payments, therefore you can be sure that no past activity will ever be removed and go unnoticed.

If your group link is compromised, you can create a new group and enter the starting balances.

Privacy

We do not use tracking and session cookies. We also don't have an account system and don't log IP addresses. Therefore, we can't track you in any way.
